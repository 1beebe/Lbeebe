About

I'm Ligaya Beebe — an instructional designer, AI practitioner, and program manager based in Estero, Florida. I'm the founder of LBeebe Consulting LLC and co-founder of 10xJobs.co, a personal AI executive coach built by job-seekers, for job-seekers.

Before AI, I spent nearly a decade running U.S. State Department-funded cultural exchange programs across 12+ countries. My job was to take people completely outside their comfort zone — new languages, new food, new ways of thinking — and help them understand that the disorientation wasn't a problem to solve. It was the learning. The culture shock, the struggle, the humility of not knowing: that's where growth actually happens. I think about AI adoption the same way.

Most AI training is either too abstract ("here's what a neural network does") or too prescriptive ("here are 10 ChatGPT prompts"). Neither creates lasting change. I work differently: I start with what your team actually does, identify where AI creates real impact, and build fluency from there.

"The disorientation isn't a problem to solve. It's where the learning happens."

I'm a MindStudio Level 3 certified AI agent developer and an active builder — the tools I recommend are tools I use daily. I'm also a community educator: I run the AI Architect Program inside Never Search Alone, a free peer-learning series that has trained 91+ professionals with zero paid marketing.
