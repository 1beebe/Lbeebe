About

I'm Ligaya Beebe — an instructional designer, AI practitioner, and program manager based in Estero, Florida. I'm the founder of L. Beebe LLC and co-founder of 10xJobs.co, a personal AI executive coach built by job-seekers, for job-seekers.

Before AI, I spent years doing a version of this same work in much harder conditions. I taught at universities in Qinghai Province — one of the most remote parts of China, home to Tibetan and Hui Muslim communities most people couldn't find on a map. Before that, I was a New York City Teaching Fellow, put in front of public school classrooms with minimal support and high stakes. I've spent my career helping people learn things they were convinced they couldn't — across language barriers, cultural barriers, and the particular barrier of not believing the thing was meant for someone like them.

I think about AI adoption the same way. Most people who struggle with AI aren't struggling because they're not smart enough. They're struggling because nobody designed the learning for them. That's a solvable problem — and it's the one I've been solving my whole career.

"Most people who struggle with AI aren't struggling because they're not smart enough. Nobody designed the learning for them."

I hold an MS in Special Education alongside graduate training in Social Sciences — which means I understand how people learn when the standard approach isn't working. I'm a MindStudio Level 3 certified AI agent developer and an active builder: the tools I recommend are tools I use and build with daily. I also run the AI Architect Program inside Never Search Alone, a free peer-learning series that has trained 91+ professionals with zero paid marketing.
