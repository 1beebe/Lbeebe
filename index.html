About

I'm Ligaya Beebe, founder of L. Beebe LLC and co-founder of 10xJobs.co, a personal AI executive coach built by job-seekers, for job-seekers.

Before I pivoted to AI, I spent nine years at iEARN-USA building a career in learning and development at every level of an organization. As a Coordinator I was the front-line person helping students and families navigate the disorientation of overseas programs. When I became a Program Manager I shifted to developing the people around me: coaching direct reports and building the capacity of our international implementing partners. By the time I reached Senior Grants and Program Manager, I was operating at the relationship and systems level, managing U.S. government funders and creating the conditions for my team to thrive.

Prior to that, I taught at universities in Qinghai Province (one of the most remote parts of China) and was a New York City Teaching Fellow in under-resourced public schools. I've spent my career helping people learn things they were convinced they couldn't.

AI adoption is the same problem in a new context. Most people who struggle with it aren't struggling because they're not smart enough; nobody designed the learning for them. That's the kind of problem I've been solving for decades.

The tools I recommend are tools I build with daily, mostly in MindStudio or with Claude Code. I also run the AI Architect Program inside Never Search Alone, a free peer-learning series, 91+ professionals, zero paid marketing. When I'm not wrangling two kids, I try to fill my sketchbook and photograph Florida wildlife — and sometimes I actually do.
